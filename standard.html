Clinical scope

Adult inpatient default model only. Hard stops: pediatric patients, MIC ≥2 mg/L, renal replacement therapy, ECMO, unstable renal function/AKI, or samples drawn during infusion. This app is for pharmacist-supervised TDM and institutional validation only.

This software is provided as a calculation framework. It is not certified as a medical device and does not replace clinical judgement, local antimicrobial stewardship policy, or validated commercial/institutional Bayesian dosing software.
